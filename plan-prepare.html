Planning and preparing for Screen/Pick-N-Roll

Planning stage starts as soon as you turn towards opposition basket. By the time you reach halfway line you should see your options available for setting up.

Starting

  • Look for any opposition player you can pick out or slow down
  • Identify the defender to target based on their game
  • Understand defence pattern like, guarding key, push out, high line etc
  • Where is faster, taller defender positioned.
  • Always play as pair

    • Create distance between defenders, ideally set pick on defender with back to you.

    When key is defended

    Use High-Low Pick or Low-High Pick method to set it up.

    When defending high line

    • Player1 take defender to sideline or as far they will follow
    • At same time player 2 take defender toward middle creating gap.
    • Depending on positioning and defend
