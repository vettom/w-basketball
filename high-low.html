Player at Higher position pick

  • Player 1 fake a move to turn back of defender
  • Player 2 fake move and turns around to pick for 1
  • Player 1 moves to top
  • If defender follow Player 1, player 2 goes for Basket
  • If defender stays with player 2, Player 1 goes for basket.

    • Ideally faster player pick for slower player
