Pick-N-Roll

Pick and roll involves more space and ideal for players with wider range for making shots. If the shooter does not have high percentage shooting, defenders may not see them as threat and move will not happen. Also shooter must ensure to keep defender engaged for the pick to happen as there is much more space involved for defender to recover.

Setting up

  • Pick position between defenders
  • Shooter position near 3 point line based on which player is allowe to jump
  • Shooter receive ball and take aim to score

    • Trigger

  • Onve of the defenders moves out to stop the shot
  • Shooter keeps defender engaged, ideally with back towards pick
  • Player picking moves out and sets pick
  • Shooter moves in the direction of Pick
  • Pick moves in opposite direction, keeping defender behind
  • Pass ball to player best positioned
    • To be where you have never been before, you have to do what you have never done before!
